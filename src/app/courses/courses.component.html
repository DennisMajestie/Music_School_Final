BEGINNER

  • Introduction to music theory and instrument basics
  • Development of fundamental skills, such as hand positioning, finger placement, and basic techniques
  • Simple songs and exercises to build confidence and familiarity
INTERMEDIATE

  • Expansion of technical skills, including scales, arpeggios, and chord progressions
  • Introduction to more complex repertoire, including classical and contemporary pieces
  • Focus on musical expression, phrasing, and dynamics
ADVANCE

  • Refining techniques, such as tone production, articulation, and pedaling (for pianists)
  • Exploration of advanced repertoire, including intricate compositions and nuanced interpretations
  • Emphasis on performance skills, including stage presence and audience engagement
PROFICIENT

  • Mastery of instrument and musical style, with a focus on subtlety and finesse
  • Development of personal expression and interpretation, with a deep understanding of the music
  • Preparation for professional performances, auditions, or music competitions
