About Us

A MUSIC SCHOOL PAR EXCELLENCE

The Eagles Music Academy is a pacesetter, a beacon of light, a trailblazer, and a groundbreaking music school whose task is simple: - Unfold the latent talent.
With a proven track record of excellence, the academy has received prestigious invitations to perform nationwide over the past seven years. Notable performances which include:

  • The Amnesty International Fiesta
  • The National Christmas Carol (NCC)
  • The FRCN/NTA/VON Carol
  • The State House Presidential Villa

At The Eagles Music Academy, commitment to excellence is more than just a mantra – it's our academy's melody, which harmonizes with our slogan – unfolding the latent talent.

Facilities

Features modern and spacious facilities that can accommodate private instruction as well as group and ensemble training. An optimal teaching environment which contributes significantly to student learning and achievement.

Professional Tutors

Our tutors at Eagles Music Academy are professionals with towering music experience. Dedicated instructors passionate about teaching and mentoring. Committed to helping students achieve their musical goals

Small Class Size

We operate with a 5:1 student-teacher ratio, ensuring each student receives proper attention ensuring that each student receives: - Personalized attention - Tailored instruction and feedback - Increased engagement

Confidence-building

Our confidence-building lessons, helps empower your child to - Overcoming stage-fright and shyness - Boost academic achievement - Enhance communication skills Lesson Scope includes: 1. Stage Presence 2. Public Speaking

Curriculum Aims

Our curriculum is designed to - Build confidence: Performance opportunities and constructive feedback - Develop musical skills: Technique, theory, and performance. - Foster creativity: Composition, improvisation, and self-expression

Activities

A range of engaging activities, including: 1. Instrumental lessons
2. Vocal training
3. Music theory
4. Ensemble performances
5. Workshops and masterclasses
6. Recitals and concerts
